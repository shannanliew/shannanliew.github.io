Big Data

I analyzed chicken price data across UK regions, calculating average, minimum, and maximum prices. Using the cleaned dataset, I created visualizations including a bar chart to compare average prices and a stacked bar chart to show price ranges (min to max), highlighting regional disparities in chicken costs.

Python Notebook

Bar Chart Showing Average Price by Region

It shows that London has the highest average chicken prices, followed by East Anglia and the South East.

Stacked Bar Chart Showing Price Range (Max - Min) Across Regions

London has the widest price range, with a notably higher maximum price compared to other regions. In contrast, regions like East Midlands and Yorkshire & Humber display narrower price ranges, indicating relatively stable and lower chicken costs.

Data Scraping

In week 5, the challenge is to implement my own data scraper and scrape data from a source of my choice and present the data in a chart.

Python Notebook to Scrape World Population Review Data

In order to scrape our data from World Population Review for obesity rates, I have used pandas and beautifulsoup libraries to sort the necessary data needed for our analysis of the most obese countries.

After extracting and cleaning the data, I exported the tidy data to a csv file.

Top 20 Countries by Obesity Rate

After we get our tidy data, we use vega-lite transform to sort the countries in descending order, and apply ranks to them.

Note: only the top 20 countries are shown as if all countries is included this will make the chart too messy.
